How it Works:

Whether you're a donor or someone in search of baby items, creating an account is the first step to becoming a part of our community.


Donors can easily list their gently used baby items for donation. Whether it's clothing, toys, strollers, or other essentials, our intuitive listing process makes it simple for users to provide details about the items they wish to donate.


Families in need can explore our platform to find a wide range of baby items available for free. Once a user finds an item they need, they can submit a request for it. This initiates a connection between the donor and the recipient, allowing for communication and coordination.
